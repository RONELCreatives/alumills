Alucobond Cladding

Providing a great look to your building with unique colors requires the use of a rather complex panel harmonizing two aluminium cover sheets and a mineral-filled polymer core. This unique characteristic of alucobond provides a whole new inspiration to the surrounding buildings or the environment. Because of our experience, we look out for unique custom colors which fits your projects, whether is a corporate office or headquarters, private home, a public building or an industry. Our projects on Alucobond is generally used for branding, corporate buildings and structures such as banks, data centers, business centers, restaurants, and fuel stations.
