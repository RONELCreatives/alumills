Our History; the true reflection of our experience and pride speak for themselves.

ALUMILLS IMPRESSIONS LTD is a licensed contractor registered with the Ministry of Works and Housing in the category of p(D3, K3). ALUMILLS offers a wider range of services that is comparable to most construction firms in Ghana and across the globe.

Since 1999, we have moved with the waves of success; aligning our work to our values, mission and goals. We have been engaged in construction, engineering and management of more complex advanced projects for the private or corporate establishment, districts and the government. Our role in these kinds of the venture has secured us a place in our client’s hearts. We do offer to consult in addition to all the services we offer. The company recognizes the needs of the industrialized building system. This system offers unlimited flexibility in design with high-quality finish and installation speed.
